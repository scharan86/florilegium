**Core Idea**: The total "value" in the game is fixed and nothing is created or destroyed, just transferred between players. 

>[!definition]
>A zero-sum game is a situation where a player's gain is equivalent to another player's loss and the net benefit is zero. 

In a zero-sum game, no cooperation is possible as there is a complete conflict of interest between players. 

$$ \sum gain \ + \sum loss = 0$$
