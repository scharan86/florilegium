# Databases

> A database is an organized collection of structured information or data that models some aspect of the real world (abstraction of reality). 

- It's controlled by a [[DBMS]]. 
- It lets us persist, manipulate and retrieve data reliably.

A database is primarily concerned about two things: 
1. How data is stored ([[Data Models]])
2. How data is queried ([[Query Model]])

## Major Types of DBs
1. [[Relational Databases]]
2. [[NoSQL]]
3. [[Graph Databases]]
4. [[In-Memory Databases]]
5. Vector Databases
6. Time-Series Databases (TSDB)
7. Spatial and Geographic Databases
